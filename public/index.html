INSTRUCTIONS

If you don't know how Guess Who works, you will need 2 people in order to play. Ask a friend (or enemy) to get the game on their device, and play through a Discord call or something. Each player needs their own game on their own screen.

Once you're set, take turns asking "yes" or "no" questions to try and figure out who your opponent's character is! Whoever guess correct first wins.

Here are some RULES to spice up the game!

(You can adjust to your liking)

  1. No repeated questions! Once a question is asked, it can't be asked again by either player!
  2. No questions about the card's placement on the board! (Example: "Is your character's card on the top row?")
  3. Each player gets 3 guesses. If they run out of guesses, they lose! Guessing also takes a turn, so you can't ask a question and then guess right away. Gotta wait your turn.
  4. As an optional house rule, you can make it so the loser is allowed one final guess after losing, so if they manage to guess correctly, the round can end on a tie!

As a fun HARD MODE alternative, try having a game where you're only allowed to ask subjective questions, without the "yes" or "no" limit. (Example: "What's your character's favorite Pokemon?")
